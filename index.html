Welcome to » The Map « for AlphaBounce !!!

This is the first class navigation system for all prisoners and rebels in the Zonkerian System. You can find out, how many minerals the levels on your route have got and can decide, if it is worth to visit them.

Most of the mission subjects of both sides, ESCORP and FURI are marked on this map. You can also find all missiles and can see, what items the shops have to offer

To see the details of a level, simply hover with your mouse over it. If data is available, it will be shown at the right side of the map. To 'scroll' on this map, click on a random point. The new center of the map will be the point you clicked at.

There are two layers: The space and the planetary. The planetary layer shows you all information about the landing sites of all planets and most asteriods. So you can easily find all PID-Pieces, crystals and inhabitants, who need a 'taxi'.

At the moment, this map contains information about 198987 space levels. If you want to provide us with more screenshots, you can use our upload-terminal for them.
